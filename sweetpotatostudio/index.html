Witchgame: Magical Battle Arena

TBD, 2021

WitchGame: Magical Battle Arena is a Dungeon Multiplayer Twin-Stick Shooter where the Players, represented as Witches, compete in a dungeon against each other while avoiding trap and environmental change event. The winner will be the one who collects orbs from the dungeons, from killing other players or Killing a Guardian entity that lurks in the dungeons. Exploration is key, and shooting each other will be unavoidable. These two genres alone, adventure and action shooter, already tops the market as the most played genre. Players can also gain advantages by buying equipment such as pet, but individual skill and game-sense will be the major determining factor.
